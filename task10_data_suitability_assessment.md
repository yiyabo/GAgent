## 3. Conclusion

**The input data is unsuitable for mutational signature analysis with Sigflow.**

### Reason
Mutational signature analysis (Sigflow, SigProfiler, deconstructSigs) requires **DNA-level somatic variant calls** in VCF or MAF format. These files contain validated somatic SNVs/indels derived from whole-genome or whole-exome sequencing with matched tumor-normal pairs.

The available data consists entirely of **single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq)** expression matrices from GEO datasets GSE184880 and GSE292661. scRNA-seq data:

- Measures **transcript abundance**, not DNA sequence variation
- Cannot reliably detect somatic SNVs due to low per-cell coverage, allelic dropout, and reverse-transcription errors
- Is confounded by RNA editing events (A-to-I, C-to-U) that mimic somatic mutations
- Lacks the matched normal DNA required to distinguish somatic from germline variants
